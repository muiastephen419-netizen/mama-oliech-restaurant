Our Story

Where Every Meal
Feels Like Home

✦

Mama Oliech Restaurant was born from a simple belief — that the best food comes from the heart. Since opening our doors in 2008, we have been serving Nairobi the flavours it grew up loving: slow-cooked stews, hand-pounded ugali, freshly grilled nyama choma and fragrant coastal pilau.

Every recipe has been passed down, perfected, and served with pride. This is not just a restaurant — it is a gathering place for families, friends and anyone who believes a great meal can make any day better.
