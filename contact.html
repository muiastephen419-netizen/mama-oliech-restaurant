Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

✦
Do I need a reservation? +
Walk-ins are always welcome! However, we recommend reserving a table on weekends and public holidays as we fill up quickly. Reservations are required for groups of 8 or more.
Do you offer takeaway and delivery? +
Yes! We offer takeaway for all menu items. Call us or WhatsApp your order 30 minutes in advance and it will be ready for collection. We also partner with Bolt Food and Glovo for delivery within Nairobi.
Can you cater for private events? +
Absolutely. We cater for birthdays, weddings, corporate events, funerals and any gathering. Our team will work with you to create the perfect menu. Contact us at least 3 days in advance for event bookings.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, we have free parking available for all our guests. Our parking area is secure and monitored.
Do you have vegetarian or halal options? +
Yes! We have several vegetarian-friendly dishes on our menu including githeri, sukuma wiki, rice and beans and more. All our beef and chicken dishes are halal. Please let us know of any dietary requirements when you book.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept M-Pesa (our most popular method), cash, and major debit/credit cards. M-Pesa Till Number: 123456.
